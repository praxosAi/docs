## Obtaining Your API Key

### Step 1: Access Your Praxos Account
Navigate to the Praxos platform at [https://data.praxos.ai](https://data.praxos.ai) and log in using your credentials.

### Step 2: Navigate to Account Settings
1. Locate your profile menu in the bottom left corner of the application
2. Click on your name/email to expand the menu
3. Select "Account" from the dropdown options

### Step 3: Retrieve Your API Key
1. In the Account settings page, locate the "Change Password" section
2. Your API key will be displayed in this section
3. To view the key, click the eye icon to reveal the hidden characters
4. Copy and securely store your API key
